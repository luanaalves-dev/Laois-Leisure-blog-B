Swimming Lessons

Laois Leisure provides superb 25 meter swimming pools. Whether you are an experienced swimmer or a novice the pool is set up for you with designated lanes throughout the day so you can get the best out of your swimming experience. We offer a range of activities such as child lessons, adult lessons, inflatable sessions, aquafit, lane swims and public swims.Please see timetables below for both our Portlaoise and Portarlington.

Children's Swimming For All Levels

Little swimmers (3-4 years old)

Focus on teaching your child new skills such as water confidence & how to move in water. Intro to being prone and supine.

Level 5

For children with ability to swim 9m freestyle & backstroke, knowledge of breaststroke & tread water for 30sec.

Level 1

For children who can take instruction, with the ability to kick on front and back with 2 boards. Must be able to get their face wet and blow bubbles.

Level 6

For children with the ability to kick 9m (1 width), freestyle & backstroke, and breaststroke with good techniques. Swim 18m any stroke with no support.

Level 2

For children with the ability to kick 5m on front with 1 board putting face down. Must be able to kick 3m on them with 1 board.

Level 7

For swimmers able to swim 25m freestyle, backstroke & have an understanding of breaststroke and butterfly. Be able to tread water.

Level 3

For children with the ability to kick 5m with or without an aid, put face in the water and stand in 1m depth of water.

Level 8

For swimmers able to swim 50m freestyle, backstroke & breaststroke and have good understanding of butterfly.

Level 4

For children with ability to kick 9m on front and back with an arm action and have an understanding of front crawl breathing.

Rookie Lifeguard

Rookie lifeguards will develop skills & knowledge in water safety, rescue, resuscitation and lifesaving skills.

Parent & Baby Swimming Classes

At Laois Leisure, we believe it’s never too early to introduce your child to the water. Our Parent & Baby swimming classes are specially designed to help parents and their little ones (from 3 months up) build confidence in the water together in a safe, fun, and supportive environment. Classes take place in our dedicated learner pool a smaller, warmer, and more affordable space ideal for babies and toddlers to feel comfortable. Led by qualified instructors, each session features playful water-based activities that promote bonding, sensory development, and early swim skills. Whether you're a new parent or returning to the pool, our facilities are here to support your family’s swimming journey. Check our Portlaoise and Portarlington timetables for availability.

Adult Swim Lessons

Swimming is one of the best full-body workouts-gentle on the joints, great for heart health, and ideal for building strength, flexibility, and stamina. Whether you're new to the water or an experienced swimmer, Laois Leisure is here to support your goals. Our high-quality 25-metre pools are open daily with designated lanes, giving you the freedom to swim at your own pace in a calm and structured environment. From improving fitness and reducing stress to helping with injury recovery or simply enjoying time to yourself, swimming offers a wide range of physical and mental health benefits.

We offer a variety of adult swimming classes, including beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. Our experienced instructors will work with you to develop your skills, whether you're looking to improve your technique, build endurance, or learn new strokes. Classes are available at various times throughout the week, so you can find a schedule that fits your lifestyle. Check our Portlaoise and Portarlington timetables for availability.

Feel Stronger and Energised with AquaFit at Laois Leisure

Looking for a fun, low-impact way to get in shape and feel great? Aquafit is the perfect fitness class for all ages and abilities that combining the resistance of water with dynamic, easy-to-follow movements that tone your body and boost your energy. Because it’s water-based, AquaFit is gentle on your body, making it ideal for those with joint issues, recovering from injury, or simply looking for a fun, social way to stay active. Best of all, there’s no need to sweat at the gym!

Classes are upbeat, welcoming, and set to music, so you’ll leave feeling rejuvenated, refreshed, and motivated. Whether you're new to exercise or looking to mix up your routine, dive into AquaFit and experience fitness the fun way!

At Laois Leisure, we are committed to working towards a healthier community.
